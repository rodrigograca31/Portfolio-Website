"Declare your love" (Translated title) was a Facebook app that aggregated all the videos under the #ControlDiaDosNamorados2016 Instagram hashtag.
There was also a back-office for the brand to manage and choose the winner video.
The winner then won an iPhone 6S and other brand-related items.
