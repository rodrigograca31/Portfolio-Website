Description:

Instant Squares lets you split/splice your pictures into multiple pieces and upload them directly to Instagram to create a big square or a banner/carousel/album and amaze all your Instagram followers.

This app is an ongoing personal project that I maintain by keeping up with the underlying platform changes (Android) as well as design trends (Material UI).

My contributions:

  • Identified a gap in the market and decided to fill it.
  • Conceptualized, developed, marketed, and currently maintain it.
  • Built with Ionic/Angular 1, Cordova, and various plugins also Jquery and Sentry.

Achievements:

  • 1+ million downloads
  • Currently has hundreds of DAU (Daily Active Users).
